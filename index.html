Hello! Welcome to my home page!

I'm Phuong Ho, "uo" is pronounced as "u" in "full."

I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Centre for Applied Research at the Norwegian School of Economics. Between 1.2022 and 10.2022, I am visiting Cornell's Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management. Office: Warren Hall 409.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Arizona in 2019. My current research focuses on the intersection of IO and environmental and resource economics. For example, do firms' decisions and consumers' choices affect the environment? How do firms and consumers respond to environmental and resource policies?
